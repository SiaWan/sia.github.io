About DMH

Data and Media Hack aims to mix students from various backgrounds and apply data, media, communication, business and all the skills learnt at school to the 72 hours project, solving the most urgent problems that faced by our society, and bringing value and impact through an innovative way. You can choose one topic below and embark on your data journey!

Culture and Entertainment

Health and Aging Problem

Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence in X

You can build your idea based on film, graphics, literary arts, music, and visual / media arts. Try to come up with a new and innovative way that users either experience, consume, or create entertainment/media.

Aiming to find innovative solutions to front-line healthcare problems. Another direction is aging problems, which is quite urgent in HK and even around the world. So the other focus is to design, prototype and build solutions for supporting people in their later years of life.

Where X denotes applications such as Data-journalism, Data-healthcare, and Data-literature.

FAQS

  • What do teams work?
    • We encourage people to work in teams of up to five. If you can’t find a team beforehand, worry not - there will be time to form teams after registration. But we highly encourage to form a team with various backgrounds, because the more diverse participating teams are, the more diverse and innovative solutions will be!
  • What do I need to bring?
    • All you’ll need is a valid student ID, and your laptop & charger. We also recommend bringing some warm clothing, it gets a bit chilly at night!
  • Will there be food?
    • Yes! We offer delicious food, snacks and coffee during the three day event.
  • Who can come?
    • Any undergraduate or graduate students is eligible to enter.
  • What are the guidelines?
    • Yes we do have some guidelines on team formation and topic exploration. We will continuously update this website, so please don’t forget to check for updating information later! Or you can follow our DMC Facebook account for further updates.
  • Get a question that’s not on the list?
    • Contact us at bu-dmc@hkbu.edu.hk.
